Our Products

TMT Bars

TMT (Thermo-Mechanically Treated) bars are high-strength reinforcement bars that have a tough outer core and a soft inner core. They are commonly used in the construction industry, especially for building structures like residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects, due to their strength, durability, and flexibility.

MS Angle

MS (Mild Steel) Angles are structural steel components used in various construction and fabrication applications. Known for their versatility and strength, MS Angles have an "L" shape with two legs, which gives them a good load-bearing capacity. They are made from mild steel, which provides durability and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of structural applications.

MS Channel

MS (Mild Steel) Channels are structural steel components characterized by their C-shaped cross-section. They are widely used in construction and manufacturing due to their strength, versatility, and ease of fabrication. MS Channels are commonly used in a variety of applications where strong support and structural integrity are required.

MS Beams

MS (Mild Steel) Beams are structural steel components that are widely used in construction and engineering due to their strength, durability, and versatility. They have a cross-sectional shape that resembles an "I" or "H," and they are designed to support loads and resist bending or deformation. MS Beams are crucial in various applications, from building frames to bridges and other structures.

MS Flat

MS (Mild Steel) Flats, also known as flat bars, are versatile steel products with a flat rectangular cross-section. They are widely used in various construction, fabrication, and manufacturing applications due to their strength, ease of handling, and adaptability. MS Flats come in different sizes and thicknesses, making them suitable for a wide range of uses.

MS Round Bars

MS (Mild Steel) Round Bars, often referred to simply as round bars, are cylindrical steel rods that are widely used in construction and manufacturing. Known for their versatility, strength, and ease of fabrication, MS Round Bars have numerous applications in various industries.

MS Square Bars

MS (Mild Steel) Square Bars are steel products with a square cross-section. They are known for their versatility and strength, making them widely used in construction, manufacturing, and various engineering applications. MS Square Bars come in different sizes

Wire Rod

Wire Rod is a long, thin, rolled steel product that is used primarily as a raw material for producing wire and various other steel products. It is produced in a wide range of diameters and can be made from different grades of steel, including mild steel, carbon steel, and alloy steel. Wire Rod is characterized by its versatility and strength, making it essential in various industrial applications.

Binding Wire

Binding Wire, often referred to as binding wire or construction wire, is a type of mild steel wire commonly used in construction, agriculture, and various industrial applications. It is known for its flexibility, strength, and ease of use, making it a preferred choice for tying, binding, and securing materials together.
